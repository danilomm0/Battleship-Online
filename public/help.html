How to Play Battleship

In the game Battleship, players take turns guessing the locations of their opponent's ships. The goal is to sink all of the opponent's ships before they sink yours.

  • Place your ships on the grid.
  • Take turns guessing where your opponent's ships are located.
  • If you hit an opponent’s ship, they will report it as a "hit".
  • The first player to sink all of their opponent’s ships wins!

Account Management

To play Battleship, you need to have an account. If you don’t have one, you can easily create it by registering.

  • Login: Use your registered username and password to log in and start playing.
  • Register: If you’re new, create a new account by providing a username and password.
