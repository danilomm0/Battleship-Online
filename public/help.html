How to Play Battleship

In the game Battleship, players take turns guessing the locations of their opponent's ships. The goal is to sink all of the opponent's ships before they sink yours.

  • Drag and drop your ships onto the grid to place them in your desired locations.
  • If you'd like to save time, use the "Place Randomly" option to place ships automatically.
  • Take turns guessing where your opponent's ships are located.
  • If you hit an opponent’s ship, the square in which it is located will gray out.
  • The first player to sink all of their opponent’s ships wins!

Starting an Online Session

Want to play with a friend online? Here's how:

  • Click on the Create Lobby button.
  • A unique code will be generated for your lobby.
  • Share this code with your friend so they can join the lobby.
  • Once they enter the code, the online session will start.
  • Using your browsers back button instead of pressing back to home will cause socket issues within multiplayer. Please refresh the page if you do use your browsers back button and it starts causing errors.

AI Difficulty Levels

Play against the computer by choosing one of the following AI difficulty levels:

  • Easy: A simple AI with predictable moves. Great for beginners.
  • Medium: Offers a balanced challenge with moderately strategic moves.
  • Hard: A tough opponent with advanced strategies to test your skills.
  • Impossible: A nearly unbeatable AI with perfect decision-making.

Account Management

To play Battleship online, you need to have an account. If you don't have one, you can easily create it by registering.

  • Login: Use your registered username to log in and start playing.
  • Register: If you’re new, create a new account by providing a username.
